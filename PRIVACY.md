# Privacy Policy — Chess Coach AI

**Effective date:** April 21, 2026

## Overview

Chess Coach AI is a fully offline chess application. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection

Chess Coach AI collects **no personal data** of any kind.

- No account or sign-in is required.
- No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs are included.
- No advertising networks are integrated.
- No internet permission is declared in the app.

## Data Stored on Device

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**, using a local database (Hive):

- Game statistics (wins, losses, draws).
- Saved game states (board position, move history, difficulty level).

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party. It can be deleted by clearing the app's data in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

## Third-Party Services

Chess Coach AI does not use any third-party services, SDKs, APIs, or analytics tools.

## Children's Privacy

Chess Coach AI does not collect any information from any user, including children under 13. It is safe to use for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated policy will be included in the next app update and reflected by a new effective date above.
