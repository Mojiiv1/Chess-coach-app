--- FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars) ---
Chess Coach AI is your personal chess trainer — always available, completely offline.

Play against a powerful AI opponent at four difficulty levels, from beginner-friendly random moves to an advanced engine with deep search and opening-book knowledge. After each game, the built-in coach reviews your moves, flags blunders, identifies tactical patterns (forks, pins, hanging pieces), and explains how to improve.

FEATURES

• Four AI difficulty levels — Beginner, Easy, Intermediate, Advanced
• Real-time move evaluation bar showing position advantage at a glance
• Coach panel with actionable post-move feedback
• Tactical pattern detection: hanging pieces, forks, pins
• Save games mid-session and resume them later
• Local multiplayer — pass-and-play on one device
• Full move history in standard algebraic notation (SAN)
• Win/loss/draw statistics tracked across sessions
• 100% offline — no internet required, no account needed, no ads

PRIVACY

Chess Coach AI collects no personal data of any kind. All game data stays on your device. See the full privacy policy for details.

KEYSTORE SETUP (before building for release)
====================================================

1. Generate keystore (run this yourself — do NOT share the output):
   keytool -genkey -v \
     -keystore ~/chess-coach-release.jks \
     -keyalg RSA -keysize 2048 -validity 10000 \
     -alias chess-coach-key

2. Create android/key.properties (already in .gitignore):
   storePassword=<your-store-password>
   keyPassword=<your-key-password>
   keyAlias=chess-coach-key
   storeFile=<absolute-path-to>/chess-coach-release.jks

3. Build signed release AAB:
   flutter build appbundle --release

4. The output will be at:
   build/app/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab
